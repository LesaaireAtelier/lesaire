Frequently Asked Questions

What’s your design style?

We lean towards modern, edgy luxury — but always adapt our creativity to reflect each client’s unique personality.

Can I hire you for just design, not full execution?

Absolutely. We offer design-only packages for clients who already have contractors or want flexibility.

How long does a project usually take?

It depends on the scope, but residential projects typically take 2–6 months from concept to handover.
